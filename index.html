Why people keep coming back

Built by musicians, for anyone who's ever hummed along.

Anuraagam started as five friends jamming in a garage in Hyderabad. Today it's a community of singers, instrumentalists, and an audience that shows up not to watch a concert — but to be part of one.

  • ✔ Beginner-friendly open mic slots every session
  • ✔ Live house band — vocals, sax, guitar, percussion, keys
  • ✔ Small, intimate venues across Hyderabad
  • ✔ Every ticket funds the next artist's stage time
